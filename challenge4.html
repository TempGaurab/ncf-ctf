John's mom has the blood group AB+ and John's dad has the blood group AB+? Based on this what will be the blood group of John.
Hint

Blood type AB+ can only give alleles A and B to their children. Both parents have the same blood type, so all possible combinations will result in the same blood type. Remember to write the answer in CAPITAL LETTERS and do not forget the symbol.

Correct!
